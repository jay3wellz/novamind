FAQ

Pricing Questions

Is the free consultation really free?

Yes, completely free and no-commitment. We analyze your business and show you where automation can save time and increase revenue — with real numbers, not vague promises.

Can I add extras to any package?

Absolutely. All add-ons listed on the Services page can be added to any core package. The final price is the package price + add-ons — clearly calculated before any work begins.

How long does implementation take?

Smart websites typically take 2–4 weeks. Automation systems 3–5 weeks. The full infrastructure package 4–8 weeks. Timeline depends on scope and how quickly you can provide content and approvals.

Do I need a monthly plan after my project?

Monthly plans are optional but recommended. You keep ownership of everything we build regardless. The monthly plans ensure your systems stay optimized, updated, and continuously improving.

What if my needs don't fit any package?

Book a free consultation and we'll build a custom proposal tailored to your exact needs and budget. Custom development is billed at $125/hr with a minimum 10-hour block.
